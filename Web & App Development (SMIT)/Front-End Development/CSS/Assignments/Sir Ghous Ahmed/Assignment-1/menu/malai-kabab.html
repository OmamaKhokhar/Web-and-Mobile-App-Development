Malai Kabab

About the Dish

Malai Kabab is a creamy and succulent grilled chicken dish. It is marinated in a mixture of cream, yogurt, spices, and tenderizers, giving it a rich and melt-in-your-mouth texture. Malai Kabab is often served as an appetizer or as part of a larger meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (boneless)
  • Cream
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Green chili paste
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Garam masala
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Cashew paste
  • Butter
  • Salt to taste

Steps to Make

  1. Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and make slits on them.
  2. In a bowl, mix cream, yogurt, ginger garlic paste, green chili paste, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, kasuri methi, cashew paste, and salt.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and grill until cooked through, basting with butter for extra flavor.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney and sliced onions.

Preparation Time: 45 minutes
